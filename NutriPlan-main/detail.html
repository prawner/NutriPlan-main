Day 1

    Butter-Waffle Cookies and Mascarpone

    Recipe

    • 1 lb unsalted butter, softened
    • 3 C Brown Sugar
    • 6 Large Eggs
    • 1 tbsp Vanilla Extract
    • 1 tsp Almond Extract
    • 7 C Rouge de Bordeaux Flour
    • 1/4 tsp Baking Soda
    • 1/4 tsp Salt
    • 2 C of Heavy Cream

    Instructions

    • Cream Butter, Brown Sugar, Eggs, Vanilla, and Almond Extracts until smooth
    • In separate bowl, combine Flour, Baking Soda, and Salt
    • Lower your mixer speed and pour dry ingredients into wet ingredients until completely mixed together
    • Cover and chill dough for at least 1 hour
    • Preheat waffle iron
    • Place 1 tbsp of the dough into each section, close the waffle iron lid
    • When browned, place the cookies on a rack to cool
    • In a saucepan, bring the Heavy Cream to a steady 180° F for 3 minutes
    • After 3 minutes at 180 F, add the Lemon Juice, stir, and keep the mixture at 180 F for an additional 3 minutes
    • Line a strainer with 3 layers of cheesecloth then set into a glass bowl, pour in cooled mixture over the cheesecloth
    • Once your cheese has firmed to a spreadable consistency remove from cheesecloth
    • This cheese is meant to be piped onto the cookie
